About me:

Welcome to my personal landing page in the web!
My name is Martin Jung and I am an aspiring next-generation ecologist.

I did my BSc Thesis in Biology at the University of Marburg, Germany, in which I investigated the response of temperate plant-pollinator networks towards forest fragmentation. Afterwards I moved to Denmark, where I wrote my MSc thesis at the Center for Macroecology, Evolution and Climate investigating the congruence between a broad-scale biodiversity model and independent field data. This september I am going to start a PhD in Environmental Science at the University of Sussex, United Kingdom.

I am broadly interested in Ecology and Conservation and have a personal taxonomic background in ornithology and entomology. My current research is focussed on the interplay between macroecology and natural resource management in the face of global change.

This landing page is generated by Jekyll and Bootstrap and currently runs on Github. Find a link to my repository at the bottom and free to fork this and other code examples from my repository!
